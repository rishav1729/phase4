About Us

For the past 30+ years, the Joy Wallace team has been creating experiences that delight our guests with innovative cuisine, creative design and flawless service. When planning an event or wedding in Florida, you need a professional caterer team who can create magical experiences. Our Miami catering menu are centered around diverse and explosive flavors, fresh local menus with global flair, and sparking joy with every single bite. You can put your trust in Joy Wallace to create an unparalleled experience for you and your guests every time. Our unmatched creativity and passion for culinary arts and catered events have won us awards from the International Caterers Association, Event Solutions Magazine, BizBash, Catersource, International Live Events Association, WeddingWire, South Florida Business Journal and many more. As a top Miami caterer, we love every kind of event, and we live and breathe bringing memorable moments to life. Whether you’re planning an intimate milestone birthday event at your loved one’s favorite venue, a gala for thousands to raise funds for your non-profit or your fairytale Miami wedding, our team is ready to help. Our goal is to make your event planning experience stress-free. Our team is a family, and every client who caters an event with our skilled team becomes a part of that family. We connect with you to understand your “why” and your event vision from our very first meeting. Then, we work with you to bring that to life! We know just what to do to pick a venue that’s the perfect canvas for your event, dream up a creative catering menu that pleases your palate, design a setting that will wow and impress and execute your event without issues—all so you can sit back, relax and enjoy planning your event, stress-free.

Our Mission

Stress-free menu and incredible food are what matter most at San Francisco corporate and special events. At Above & Beyond, we understand that your busy lifestyle doesn’t always have time for hours of coordinating. Our professional team provides quick, effortless planning and helps you arrange a flavorful menu that will make your event an absolute success.
